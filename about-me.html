Bio

I’m Giuseppe, an Italian scholar living in Switzerland since I moved here for my PhD. My path has mostly unfolded within universities: studying, researching, teaching, and doing the kind of service work that quietly keeps academic life running. Over time I’ve gotten used to moving between places and institutions, and I’ve come to like the feeling of learning a new context from the inside.

I’m a continuous learner by temperament. Although my training is in economics and my research experience sits in the behavioral sciences, my reading life is often philosophical and frequently multilingual. I’m drawn to questions that last, and I try to work on problems that feel relevant beyond the moment, even if progress is incremental.

Right now I’m focused on finishing my PhD. Starting 1 April 2026, I’m open to scientific roles both inside and outside academia. Outside work, I’m an experimental cook, a traveler when schedules allow, and a devoted listener of good music. This page is where I share a few links and a set of playlists for anyone who feels like listening.
