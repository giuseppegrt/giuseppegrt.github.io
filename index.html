Current research focus

01 Persuasive visualization design Visual Design Features as Persuasive Cues

How features of real interactive visualizations shape perceptions, attitudes, and online behavior.

02 Visualized uncertainty When Visualized Uncertainty Reduces and Increases Belief Update

How uncertainty displays can reduce processing fluency while increasing trust among more numerate audiences.

03 Visualization-based beliefs The Impact of Data Visualization on Beliefs and Opinions

How experimental and quasi-experimental evidence maps the effects of data visualization on beliefs and opinions.
